PRIVACY POLICY

Protecting your farm data and decision support insights.

AgroSense is a Smart Agriculture Decision Support System for crop recommendation, price prediction, and market price analysis. We are committed to handling your information responsibly so you can trust the platform every step of the way.

Introduction

AgroSense delivers intelligent farming guidance for crop recommendation, price forecasting, and market price analysis. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information needed to power those services.

Information We Collect

  • User inputs: farm details, soil conditions, crop preferences, and other data you provide to receive tailored recommendations.
  • Farm and location data: field size, soil type, climate inputs, and region-specific factors used for accurate crop and market guidance.
  • Usage data: interactions with the platform, feature use, page views, and system performance metrics.
  • Analytics data: non-identifying information collected to improve reliability, user experience, and the quality of forecasting models.

How We Use Information

  • Deliver data-driven crop recommendations based on local conditions and historical performance.
  • Generate reliable price prediction results using current market signals and historical trends.
  • Support ongoing market price analysis so you can plan planting and selling decisions with confidence.
  • Improve platform performance, troubleshoot issues, and deliver updates that keep AgroSense responsive and accurate.

Data Storage and Security

We store your data securely and limit access to authorized personnel only. Security measures include encrypted transmission, secure storage, and monitoring practices designed to protect farm inputs, forecasts, and account information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share information when it is necessary to deliver the service, when required by law, or with trusted third parties who support our platform.

Any third-party service providers are selected carefully and are required to safeguard the information they handle on our behalf.

Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and similar technologies to improve site navigation, analyze usage patterns, and support core functionality. This helps us understand how users interact with AgroSense and identify opportunities to improve the experience.

User Rights

  • Access your personal data and review how it is being used.
  • Correct or update inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of data when it is no longer necessary for the service.
  • Withdraw consent for certain data processing activities where applicable.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as needed to provide AgroSense services and comply with legal obligations. When data is no longer required, we remove or anonymize it in a safe and controlled manner.

Third-Party Services

AgroSense may rely on third-party providers for hosting, analytics, email delivery, and system maintenance. These providers are chosen for their strong security practices and are only granted access to the data necessary to perform their services.

Disclaimer

AgroSense provides decision support tools for crop recommendation, price prediction, and market price analysis. Forecasts and recommendations are based on available data and models, but they are not a guarantee of future outcomes. Always consider local conditions and consult trusted advisors when making agricultural decisions.
